---
title: Creating a New Documentation Page
description: Step-by-step guide for adding a new page.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Log in and open your main dashboard at `https://bensmith.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Page'">
    Find and click the <b>New Page</b> button in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in the title and content for your page. You can use Markdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Publish">
    Hit <b>Save</b> to create a draft, or <b>Publish</b> to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
