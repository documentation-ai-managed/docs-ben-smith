---
title: Page Not Showing Up
description: Resolve missing documentation pages.
---

## Symptoms

- New page does not appear in documentation list
- Can't access page by direct URL

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the dashboard">
    Try reloading your browser window to update the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check page status">
    Confirm your page is published, not in draft mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cache and cookies if issues persist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
